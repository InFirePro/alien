A Modern Take on the 1978 Classic

🎮 Controls

  • ← →: Move Ship
  • Space: Shoot
  • P: Pause
  • R: Restart (Game Over)

🎯 Pro Tips

  • Use barriers for cover
  • Target bottom row aliens first
  • Hit the UFO for bonus points
  • Keep moving to avoid alien shots

👾 Fun Facts

  • Aliens speed up as you destroy them
  • Original game used just 2KB of RAM
  • Generated over $600M by 1982
  • Inspired movies like "Pixels"

🏆 World Records

  • Highest Score: 1,006,600 points
  • Longest Single Game: 38 hours
  • Most Cabinets Sold: 360,000 units

📜 History

Space Invaders, created by Tomohiro Nishikado in 1978, revolutionized gaming as one of the first shoot 'em up games. It sparked the arcade golden age and caused a coin shortage in Japan.

  • First game with high score tracking
  • Pioneered the shoot 'em up genre
  • Inspired countless modern games
